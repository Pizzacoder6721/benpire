About the Benpire

The Benpire is an empire run by a few individuals. The main person who runs it is Grand Benperor Ben.

The Benpire currently holds control over parts of the Gym and Mr. Smiley's Room. The Gym is an extremely beneficial area due to its large space and the high amount of traffic it receives during games.

The Benpire is currently attempting to take over Mr. Walter's Room due to its abundance of resources. We plan to either purchase the room outright or form an alliance — for having Mr. Walter as an ally would grant us access to all his resources (including hydrogen bombs), which would prove most advantageous.
